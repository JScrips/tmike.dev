## The Classics

**"Let's circle back on this"**  
 I don't want to deal with this right now, and I'm banking on you forgetting about it entirely.  


**"I'll ping you offline"**  
I don't want witnesses for this conversation.

## The Passive-Aggressive Tier

**"Per my last email..."**  
** Learn to fucking read.  

**"I want to make sure we're aligned"**  
** You're about to do something I don't approve of, and I need to stop you without explicitly telling you no.  

**"Let's be mindful of..."**  
You're doing something wrong, but I'm too polite/scared/corporate to say it directly.  

**"That's a great point, but..."**  
That's a terrible point and here's why you're wrong.

## The Gaslighting Collection

**"We're all wearing multiple hats here"**  
 We're understaffed and you're about to do three jobs for one salary.  

**"We're a family here"**  
 We're going to guilt trip you into unpaid overtime and emotional labor.  
<sub>P.S: Families don't lay you off when Q4 projections miss.</sub> 

**"This is a growth opportunity"**  
This is going to suck, but we're rebranding it as career development.  

**"We value work-life balance"**  
We will absolutely message you at 9 PM and expect a response.

## The Meeting Menaces

**"Let's put a pin in that"**  
 I'm not saying no, but I'm also never saying yes.

**"Can everyone see my screen?"**  
 I haven't prepared for this meeting and I'm stalling.  

**"I'll have to check with my team"**  
 I have the authority to decide this right now, but I want plausible deniability later.  

**"Let's timebox this discussion"**  
 I'm about to shut down this conversation regardless of whether we've solved anything.

## Tech Talk

**"This is a known issue"**  
This bug is old enough to vote and we're never fixing it.  

**"We're refactoring for maintainability"**  
The original code was such a dumpster fire that we have to rewrite it, but we can't admit that publicly.


**"This follows our coding standards"**
I read the linter config once in 2019.  

**"It's a simple change"**
I have no idea how complex this actually is.

## Politics

**"That's above my pay grade"**  
 I know the answer but I'm not risking my neck to tell you.  


**"I'll loop in [senior person]"**  
 I need backup because this is about to go sideways.  


**"We need to be strategic about this"**  
 I'm about to deprioritize your thing for something I care about more.

**"Let's align with leadership on this"**  
 I'm not making this decision because someone higher up might disagree and I don't want the heat.
